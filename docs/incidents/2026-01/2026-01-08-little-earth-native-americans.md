# Four Homeless Oglala Sioux Citizens Detained Near Little Earth

## Summary
At least four Oglala Sioux Tribe members, all U.S. citizens who were homeless, were detained while sheltering under a bridge near Little Earth. Unable to provide documentation. At least one held 12 hours.

## Sources
1. [ICT - Indian Country Today](https://ictnews.org/news/five-native-americans-detained-by-ice-during-ongoing-raids-in-minneapolis/)

## Victim(s)
- **Number:** At least four
- **Citizenship:** U.S. Citizens, Oglala Sioux Tribe members
- **Status:** Homeless
