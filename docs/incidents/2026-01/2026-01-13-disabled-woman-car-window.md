# Disabled Woman Dragged Through Car Window by ICE Agents

## Summary
A woman claiming to be disabled was pulled out of her car by eight ICE agents after they smashed her window. She was reportedly trying to drive past an ICE operation to reach a doctor's appointment when agents blocked her path and forcibly removed her from her vehicle.
