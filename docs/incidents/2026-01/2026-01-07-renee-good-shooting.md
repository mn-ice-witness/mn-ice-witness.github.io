# Renee Nicole Good Shot and Killed by ICE Agent

## Summary
Renee Nicole Good, a 37-year-old U.S. citizen, was fatally shot by ICE agent Jonathan Ross after she stopped her SUV in the street near where agents were conducting immigration enforcement. Good had just dropped her 6-year-old son off at school.

## Victim(s)
- **Name:** Renee Nicole Good
- **Age:** 37
- **Occupation:** Poet, mother
- **Citizenship:** U.S. Citizen
- **Background:** Lived with her family a few blocks from where she died. Moved to Twin Cities from Kansas City area. City leaders described her as a legal observer of federal actions.

## Timeline
- **Morning** - Good drops her 6-year-old son off at school
- **Shortly after** - Encounters ICE agents conducting operations near her neighborhood
- **Unknown time** - Whistle blasts from community members alert neighbors to ICE presence
- **Minutes later** - Good stops her maroon Honda Pilot sideways in street, partially blocking traffic
- **Final moments** - Agent Ross drives around her, circles on foot, approaches front of car
- **Shooting** - After agents pull door handle, Good begins driving; Ross fires three shots

## Official Accounts

### DHS/ICE Statement
DHS claimed Good "weaponized" her SUV and that agent Ross acted in self-defense, stating she was attempting to run over law enforcement officers.

### Local Officials
- **Mayor Jacob Frey:** Described the federal narrative as "garbage"
- **Governor Tim Walz:** Called on ICE to end their presence in the city
- **City officials:** Stated Good was a legal observer, not a target for arrest

## Witness Accounts
Witnesses reported Good had stopped in the middle of the street and motioned for unmarked vehicles to drive past. She was turning away from Ross when he fired. Audio from one video captures her wife Becca yelling "Drive, baby, drive!" just before the shots.

## The Shooter
- **Name:** Jonathan Ross
- **Agency:** ICE Fugitive Operations, Minneapolis
- **Background:** Joined ICE 2015. Iraq veteran (Indiana National Guard). Border Patrol agent since 2007.
- **Prior Incident:** June 17, 2025 - dragged 50 yards during arrest attempt in Bloomington

## Aftermath
- Fundraiser reached $1.5 million for Good's family
- Six federal prosecutors in Minnesota resigned January 13
- State lawsuit filed against DHS, ICE, and CBP
- 9th ICE shooting since September 2025

## Editorial Assessment
**HIGH** - Extensive documentation from multiple independent sources. Basic facts undisputed. Circumstances remain contested between federal and local accounts.
