# Honduran Man Arrested at Detroit Lakes Restaurant; Partner Documents on Video

## Summary
ICE agents arrested a Honduran man outside the China Buffet restaurant in Detroit Lakes. His partner, Grace Waawaate-Ikwe Roberts (a White Earth woman), documented the arrest on video. The detained man was taken to the ICE Field Office at Fort Snelling.

## Sources
1. Detroit Lakes Online (Jan 12, 2026): [ICE officers arrest man at Detroit Lakes restaurant on Monday](https://www.dl-online.com/news/local/ice-officers-arrest-man-at-detroit-lakes-restaurant-on-monday)
2. Valley News Live (Jan 12, 2026): [ICE presence confirmed in Detroit Lakes](https://www.valleynewslive.com/2026/01/12/ice-presence-confirmed-detroit-lakes/)

## Victim(s)
- **Origin:** Honduras
- **Relationship:** Boyfriend of Grace Waawaate-Ikwe Roberts (White Earth woman)
- **Destination:** ICE Field Office, 1 Federal Drive, Fort Snelling

## What Happened
ICE agents arrested the man in the parking lot in front of the China Buffet restaurant, located in a mini-mall at 317 Frazee Street. His partner documented the arrest and was told by an ICE agent where he would be taken.

## Broader ICE Activity in Detroit Lakes
Following the arrest:
- Agents reportedly checked several businesses including Bell Bank, Detroit Lakes Community Center, Walmart, and the Chinese buffet
- The China Buffet closed with a handwritten note: "Closed. Sorry for the Inconvenience"
- Don Pablo's Mexican Restaurant (Fergus Falls and Detroit Lakes locations) closed until further notice

## Context
This arrest was part of "Operation Metro Surge," which DHS Secretary Kristi Noem called the "largest immigration operation ever" in Minnesota. It came five days after the fatal shooting of Renee Good, amid heightened tensions statewide.
