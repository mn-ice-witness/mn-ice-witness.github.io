# Federal Agents Deploy Tear Gas at Busy Lyn-Lake Intersection

## Summary
Federal agents deployed tear gas as their convoy passed through one of Minneapolis' busiest commercial intersections at Lyndale Avenue South and West Lake Street. A large crowd had gathered around the agents, blowing whistles and shouting. Agents dropped a gas canister as they prepared to exit the scene.
