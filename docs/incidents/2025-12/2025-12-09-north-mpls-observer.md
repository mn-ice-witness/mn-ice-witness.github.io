# Citizen Arrested for Watching ICE Detain Neighbors

## Summary
Federal agents arrested a U.S. citizen who was observing ICE agents detaining her neighbors on her North Minneapolis block. The arrest highlights concerns about First Amendment rights being violated during immigration enforcement.

## Sources
1. [Federal agents arrest citizen observer watching ICE detain neighbors](https://www.mprnews.org/story/2025/12/09/federal-agents-arrest-citizen-observer-watching-ice-north-minneapolis) - MPR News

## Victim(s)
- **Name:** Not disclosed
- **Citizenship:** U.S. Citizen
- **Activity:** Observing/watching ICE operations on her block

## What Happened
A citizen observer was watching ICE agents conduct operations and detain neighbors on her North Minneapolis block when she was arrested by federal agents.

## Legal Context
Observing and recording police activity is protected by the First Amendment. Citizens have the right to film law enforcement in public spaces as long as they don't physically interfere with operations.
